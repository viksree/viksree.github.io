Vikram Sreekanti

I'm a third year Ph.D. student in the RISE Lab at U.C. Berkeley working with Professor Joe Hellerstein. In general, I'm interested in databases, data systems, and distributed systems. My current work focuses on autoscaling cloud infrastructure and data context management. Check out what we're working on here, here, and here.

I graduated from U.C. Berkeley with a B.S. in EECS in May 2015.

I occassionally write about things.
